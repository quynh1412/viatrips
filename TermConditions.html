Term & Conditions

Tour price includes
•  Sightseeing vehicle (15, 25, 35, 45 seats depending on the number of guests) as per the itinerary.
•  Round-trip air tickets SGN - DAD - SGN.
•  Checked baggage: 20 kg, carry-on baggage 7 kg/1 passenger.
•  Hotel/Resort based on a standard of 2 guests/room or 3 guests/room.
•  Listing of reference hotel systems, varying hotel names based on departure dates.
•  Da Nang equivalent to 3-star: Ibiza, Hùng Anh, Bantique, Grand Sunrise… or
•  Da Nang equivalent to 4-star: Phước Mỹ An, Đông Dương, Gemma, Seven Sea…
•  Meals as per the program.
•  Entrance tickets per the itinerary.
•  Vietnamese-speaking tour guide (Guide sees off in HCM + Guide picks up the group in Da Nang).
•  Travel insurance with a maximum compensation of 120,000,000 VND/incident.
•  Viatrips hat + Bottled water + Refreshing towels.
•  VAT tax.
Tour price excludes
•  Extra sightseeing expenses outside the program: Round-trip cable car tickets to Ba Na Hills, admission to Fantasy Park & lunch at Ba Na Hills, etc.
•  Personal expenses: meals outside the program, laundry, itinerary change/cancellation fees, flight upgrades, excess baggage fees, single room supplements, etc.
Children's ticket prices
•  Children under 5 years old: No service charge applies; parents are responsible for their care and cover any additional charges (for services based on height, etc.). Only one child under 5 years old is allowed per two adults; the second child under 5 years old will be charged according to regulations for the age range of 5 to under 12 years old and single room supplements. Airplane tickets, train fares, public transportation tickets follow the regulations set by transportation providers.
•  Children from 5 years old to under 12 years old: 75% of the adult tour price (without a separate bed). Only one child from 5 to under 12 years old is allowed per two adults; the second child and beyond will need to purchase a separate bed.
•  Children 12 years old and above: Purchase a ticket at the adult rate.
Payment conditions
•  Upon registration, a 50% deposit of the tour cost is required.
•  Full payment must be completed 7-10 days before the departure date (for regular tours) and 20-25 days before departure (for holiday tours).

Cancellation policies
After payment, if you wish to change/cancel the tour, please bring the Travel Ticket to the tour registration office to process the change/cancellation, subject to fees as per Viatrips regulations. We do not handle tour change/cancellation requests over the phone.
Cancellation terms for regular days
•  Transfer to other tours before 20 days prior to departure: No fees incurred.
•  Cancelling or transferring immediately after registration from 15-19 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 50% of the tour deposit.
•  Cancelling or transferring from 12-14 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 100% of the tour deposit.
•  Cancelling from 08-11 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 50% of the tour price.
•  Cancelling from 05-07 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 70% of the tour price.
•  Cancelling from 02-04 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 90% of the tour price.
•  Cancelling within 1 day before departure: Cancellation fee: 100% of the tour price.
Cancellation terms for holidays, Tet
•  Transfer to other tours before 30 days prior to departure: No fees incurred.
•  Cancelling or transferring immediately after registration from 25-29 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 50% of the tour deposit.
•   Cancelling or transferring from 20-24 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 100% of the tour deposit.
•  Cancelling from 17-19 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 50% of the tour price.
•  Cancelling from 08-16 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 70% of the tour price.
•  Cancelling from 02-07 days before departure: Cancellation fee: 90% of the tour price.
•  Cancelling within 1 day before departure: Cancellation fee: 100% of the tour price.
•  Tours falling on holidays or festive days are classified as those occurring on such days according to regulations.
•  Cancellation period is counted on working days, excluding Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays.
Force majeure cases
If the travel program is canceled or altered by either party due to force majeure (such as fire, weather, accidents, natural disasters, war, epidemic, delays, transportation cancellations or changes by public transport, or other events beyond legal regulations), then neither party will be obliged to compensate for any losses incurred, nor bear any legal responsibility. However, each party is responsible for making the maximum effort to assist the affected party in minimizing the resulting losses due to force majeure.
